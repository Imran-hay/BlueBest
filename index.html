Welcome!

BlueBest is a sole proprietary multi-sectoral industry investment firm that has been majorly involved in the Real estate and manufacturing businesses ever since its establishment in the year 2011 in Addis Ababa by Ethiopian young entrepreneurs. The company has a successful textile industry in Kenya. BlueBest Trading joined the pharmaceutical sector in May 2013 with the aim of importing and distributing quality pharmaceutical products, medical devices and laboratory reagents.

Our Services

Business operationsBlueBest Trading serves the Ethiopian market through import, distribution, marketing, and tendering agency. We have partnerships with renowned pharmaceutical manufacturers and seek additional suppliers globally. Import and DistributionImport and distribution of patented generic pharmaceuticals, over-the-counter medicinal products, medical devices, medical equipment. Pharmaceutical Marketing ServicesWe provide specialists, product experts, and medical workers to consult customers at points of sale, increasing brand awareness and sales. We also engage influencers, distributors, and organize promotional events to meet all customer needs in pharmaceutical advertising. Local Representation for Government TendersBlueBest Trading understands the importance of government contracts and helps partners overcome challenges by offering local representation and agency services for effective participation in international tenders. Market AuthorizationBlueBest Trading supports regulatory professionals in the medical industry by offering local expertise to navigate product registration and obtain market authorizations efficiently.

Mission.

Our mission is to provide our community with high-quality medications, medical devices, and laboratory reagents at affordable prices.
